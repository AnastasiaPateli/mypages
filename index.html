Final Project "Hyperbolic Paraboloid"

This project investigates a solid with double-ruled surfaces, leveraging industrial flat surfaces to create a hyperbolic paraboloid form. It combines geometry, design, and engineering principles to explore the potential of architectural structures.

×

International Masterclass "Hands on Particle Physics"

This project delves into particle therapy for cancer treatment. By analyzing different particles (protons, photons, and carbons), the research aimed to optimize therapy by adjusting the throw angles and the particles used, ensuring cancer cells are targeted without damaging healthy tissues.

×

Personal Project: Genetic Engineering and DNA Editing

This project was dedicated to studying CRISPR-Cas9 technology and its potential for gene editing. The goal was to design solutions for genetic diseases through 3D models and printing, illustrating the powerful impact of genetic engineering in medicine.
