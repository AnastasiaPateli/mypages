Past Research Programs

Final Project ”Hyperbolic Paraboloid” Class of ”Geometrical Illustrations” First Semester, School of Architecture, NTUA

A research team work project on a solid with double-ruled surfaces made by industrial flat surfaces.

International Masterclass ”Hands on Particle Physics”

Studied particle therapy (radiation) for cancer treatment and conducted a team-work project analyzing the effectiveness of different particles (protons, photons, carbons) using MatRad to suggest a combination of a throw angle and a particles that cure cancer cells without damaging the healthy ones

Personal Project: ”Genetic engineering and DNA editing to combat genetic diseases” MYP (Year 5), Psychico College

A 6-month research project on how CRISPR-Cas9 works and its potential applications for the treatment of genetic diseases creating a final product with 3D Design and Printing.
